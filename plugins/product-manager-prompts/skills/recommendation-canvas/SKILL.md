# AI Recommendation Canvas Skill

Helps product managers evaluate and present AI solution recommendations using a structured canvas approach. Ideal for getting executive buy-in on AI initiatives.

## When to Use

- Evaluating whether to build an AI-powered feature
- Presenting AI solution recommendations to stakeholders
- Creating business cases for AI investments
- Assessing viability, desirability, and feasibility of AI products
- Documenting AI product decisions

## Validation Checklist

When reviewing AI recommendation canvases:

### Problem & Outcome
- [ ] Business outcome is measurable and time-bound
- [ ] Product outcome focuses on user value, not features
- [ ] Problem statement is from persona's perspective
- [ ] Root cause is identified, not just symptoms

### Hypothesis & Experiments
- [ ] Hypothesis is falsifiable
- [ ] Experiments are "tiny acts" - quick and cheap
- [ ] Proof-of-life metrics are observable
- [ ] Timeframe is realistic

### Positioning
- [ ] Differentiation is defensible
- [ ] Competitor comparison is fair
- [ ] Benefits focus on outcomes

### Risks
- [ ] All 6 PESTEL categories considered
- [ ] Investigate vs Monitor distinction is clear
- [ ] Legal/compliance risks specifically called out

### Justification
- [ ] Value judgment is honest (not everything is "Absolutely yes")
- [ ] Justifications would convince a skeptical executive
- [ ] Written to obtain sponsorship, not just approval

### Success & Next Steps
- [ ] Metrics are SMART
- [ ] Next steps are actionable and assigned
- [ ] Dependencies are identified

## AI-Specific Considerations

When evaluating AI solutions, also consider:

### Data Requirements
- What data is needed to train/run the model?
- Do we have access to this data?
- What are the data quality requirements?

### Model Risks
- What happens when the model is wrong?
- How do we detect and correct errors?
- What's the feedback loop for improvement?

### Ethical Considerations
- Could this solution create bias or harm?
- How do we ensure fairness?
- What transparency do users need?

### Build vs Buy
- Can we use existing AI services?
- What's the cost of building in-house?
- What's the maintenance burden?
