# Market Analysis Skill

Helps product managers conduct comprehensive market analysis using PESTEL framework, TAM/SAM/SOM sizing, and Market Requirements Documents (MRD).

## When to Use

- Evaluating market opportunities for new products
- Assessing macro-environmental factors affecting strategy
- Sizing market potential for business cases
- Creating market requirements for stakeholder alignment
- Conducting strategic planning exercises

---

## PESTEL Analysis Template

Analyze macro-environmental factors that could impact your project/product:

```markdown

**Project/Product Name**: [Name]
**Analysis Purpose**: [Specific goal for this analysis]
**Analyst**: [Name/team]
**Date**: [Date]

### 1. Political Factors
- **Government Policies**: [Impact of policies on project/product]
- **Political Stability**: [Regional stability assessment]
- **Trade Regulations**: [Trade policy effects]
- **Taxation Policy**: [Tax implications]

### 2. Economic Factors
- **Economic Growth**: [Growth rates and impact]
- **Inflation Rate**: [Pricing and cost effects]
- **Exchange Rates**: [Currency fluctuation impact]
- **Consumer Spending**: [Spending patterns influence]

### 3. Social Factors
- **Demographics**: [Population trends affecting market]
- **Cultural Trends**: [Cultural shifts impacting demand]
- **Lifestyle Changes**: [Behavioral changes to consider]
- **Consumer Attitudes**: [Attitude shifts affecting adoption]

### 4. Technological Factors
- **Technological Advancements**: [Tech evolution impact]
- **R&D Activity**: [Innovation in the sector]
- **Automation**: [Efficiency and cost implications]
- **Digital Transformation**: [Digital trends affecting product]

### 5. Environmental Factors
- **Climate Change**: [Climate-related considerations]
- **Sustainability Practices**: [ESG requirements]

### 6. Legal Factors
- **Compliance Requirements**: [Regulatory obligations]

```

## TAM/SAM/SOM Estimation Framework

Size your market opportunity:

### Total Addressable Market (TAM)
The total market demand across the full geography/industry.

**Data Sources**:
- [Statista](https://www.statista.com)
- [IBISWorld](https://www.ibisworld.com)
- [World Bank](https://www.worldbank.org)
- [MarketResearch.com](https://www.marketresearch.com)
- [Census.gov](https://www.census.gov)

### Serviceable Available Market (SAM)
TAM segmented by region, industry, or buyer type you can realistically serve.

**Apply constraints**: Legal, regulatory, technical, channel access

**Data Sources**:
- [Eurostat](https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat)
- [Nielsen](https://www.nielsen.com)
- [Pew Research Center](https://www.pewresearch.org)

### Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)
Market share realistically capturable in 1-3 years.

**Consider**: Competition, team capacity, GTM strength, brand awareness, conversion rates, budget

**Data Sources**:
- [Crunchbase](https://www.crunchbase.com)
- [Hoover's/D&B](https://www.dnb.com)
- [Gartner](https://www.gartner.com)

### TAM/SAM/SOM Template

```markdown
## Market Size Analysis

### Total Addressable Market (TAM)
- **Description**: [Problem space being addressed]
- **Geographic Region**: [Target geography]
- **Industry/Market Segments**: [Relevant sectors]
- **Population Estimate**: [Number of potential users/buyers]
- **Market Size Estimate**: $[Amount] based on [methodology]

### Serviceable Available Market (SAM)
- **Segment of TAM**: [Specific segment targeted]
- **Constraints Applied**: [Legal, technical, geographic limits]
- **Population Estimate**: [Refined user count]
- **Market Size Estimate**: $[Amount]

### Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)
- **Realistic Share**: [% of SAM over 1-3 years]
- **Competitive Factors**: [Key competitors and positioning]
- **Population Estimate**: [Target customer count]
- **Market Size Estimate**: $[Amount]
```

## Market Requirements Document (MRD) Framework

For comprehensive market opportunity documentation:

```markdown
## Market Requirements Document (MRD)

### 1. Executive Summary
- Market opportunity overview
- Key findings and recommendations
- Business case summary

### 2. Market Overview
- Market definition and boundaries
- TAM/SAM/SOM with source citations
- Segmentation and trends
- Market trajectory (5-year scenarios)

### 3. Competitive Landscape
- Key players and positioning
- Porter's Five Forces analysis
- Pricing models and market share

### 4. Customer Analysis
- Target personas and buying behavior
- Sales cycle and procurement process
- Unmet needs and decision criteria

### 5. Problem & Solution Requirements
- Validated problem articulation
- Current solutions and gaps
- Functional, non-functional, regulatory requirements
- Success criteria and performance metrics

### 6. Business Model & Financials
- Revenue models and monetization strategies
- Investment needs and ROI expectations
- Risk factors and mitigation

### 7. Implementation & GTM Roadmap
- Go-to-market strategy
- Key milestones and timelines
- Team and resource planning
- KPIs to monitor post-launch
```

## Validation Checklist

When reviewing market analysis, verify:

### PESTEL
- [ ] All 6 factors analyzed
- [ ] Specific to the product/market, not generic
- [ ] Both opportunities and threats identified
- [ ] Actionable insights, not just observations

### TAM/SAM/SOM
- [ ] Sources cited for all estimates
- [ ] Assumptions clearly stated
- [ ] SAM constraints are realistic
- [ ] SOM is achievable, not aspirational

### MRD
- [ ] Problem validated by customer research
- [ ] Market size has credible data
- [ ] Success metrics are SMART
- [ ] Competitive analysis is thorough
- [ ] Roadmap has clear next steps
